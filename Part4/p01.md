# 📘 Chapter 12: Overfitting and Underfitting

---

## 🧠 12.1 Why This Chapter Matters

After training and evaluation, you may notice:

* Sometimes the model performs **very well on training data**
* But **poorly on test data**

👉 Why does this happen?

This is where:

> **Overfitting and Underfitting** come in

---

## ⚖️ 12.2 Two Common Problems

| Problem      | Meaning              |
| ------------ | -------------------- |
| Underfitting | Model is too simple  |
| Overfitting  | Model memorizes data |

---

## 📉 12.3 Underfitting (Model is Too Weak)

![Image](https://images.openai.com/static-rsc-4/AVTJvHAq8cmmlT0bqqtF0-j28Z8Rze1t058PEYnbg8i7KSZvaAEh7EljiF2BqjkgJCXTxSoCFanTYF1wfhwWkl-C7xlGm2WanEGiy23xl7D6EJCkCI9HjlZpaW1Qi7WBHf_sW5svqB-IHCqId4__aqbzcZ_3XFJ6f1QCdlxEMuBGF66VMJDUw7NClvuD7gTF?purpose=fullsize)

### 👉 What happens?

* Model cannot learn patterns properly
* Performs badly on:

  * Training data
  * Test data

---

### 🧠 Real-life analogy

Like a student who:

* Didn’t study enough
* Performs poorly in both practice and exam

---

### ⚠️ Signs of Underfitting

* Low accuracy everywhere
* High loss
* Model too simple

---

### 🔧 Causes

* Too few layers
* Not enough training
* Poor feature learning

---

## 📈 12.4 Overfitting (Model Memorizes Data)

![Image](https://images.openai.com/static-rsc-4/PNf2Ry-B7fZJFohHVVp0lhhxtOzLqAJfkv8tglpYhJEuv1yXZ3ZJc43g3rTNN0xa5xjjNYy5MvQ3sSBaUxNgpGhZohYuLlqxPMpYmJNuf4MM6WSHwtb0TTeOWSqAu9kDGbL_D3iQMfo9gK_spAh8qDWn2H9b_TnzJ7U6nCXSHPwPnAEB-VR7J_bZl8HLHpG6?purpose=fullsize)

### 👉 What happens?

* Model memorizes training data
* Performs well on training
* Performs poorly on test data

---

### 🧠 Real-life analogy

Like a student who:

* Memorizes answers
* Fails when questions change

---

### ⚠️ Signs of Overfitting

* High training accuracy
* Low test accuracy
* Large gap between them

---

### 🔧 Causes

* Model too complex
* Too many parameters
* Not enough data

---

## 🧠 12.6 Why CNN Can Overfit

Even CNNs:

* Have many parameters
* Can memorize training data

👉 Especially when:

* Dataset is small
* Model is deep

---

## 🔍 12.7 Bias vs Variance (Simple Idea)

* Underfitting → **High Bias**
* Overfitting → **High Variance**

---

👉 Simple meaning:

* Bias → too simple
* Variance → too sensitive

---

## 🔧 12.8 How to Fix Underfitting

### ✅ Increase model capacity

* More layers
* More filters

---

### ✅ Train longer

* Increase epochs

---

### ✅ Better features

* Improve architecture

---

## 🔧 12.9 How to Fix Overfitting

### ✅ 1. More Data

* Best solution

---

### ✅ 2. Data Augmentation

* Rotate, flip images

---

### ✅ 3. Dropout

* Randomly disable neurons

---

### ✅ 4. Reduce Model Size

* Fewer layers

---

### ✅ 5. Early Stopping

* Stop training when validation worsens

---

## 🧪 12.10 Simple Example (Concept)

```python
train_accuracy = 98
test_accuracy = 85

print("Overfitting likely")
```

## 🎯 12.12 Key Takeaways

* Underfitting → model too simple
* Overfitting → model memorizes
* Goal = balance both

---

👉 Most important idea:

> Model should **learn patterns, not memorize data**
